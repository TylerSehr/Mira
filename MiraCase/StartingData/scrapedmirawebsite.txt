SECTION 9: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How wide are the glasses?
A: They are 145.6mm (5.73 inches) wide.

Q: What does the warranty look like?
A: We offer a one-year warranty!

Q: Can I order with a prescription?
A: Yes! We ship MIRA with an option to specify your prescription. The next prescription batch ships in early April. Please note that prescription lenses are custom-made with 1.74 index lenses (the thinnest, lightest lenses available) and are non-refundable and non-reworkable once ordered.

Q: What can they do for me?
A: They can answer any question, translate any language in real time, do math instantly (e.g., 382×193), and give you perfect memory of everything you've ever talked about.

Q: What is the hardware like?
A: MIRA glasses with a dual waveguide display, microphone, and lasts 10 hours on a single charge. No camera. The display is fully private. MIRA AI listens to your day and proactively gives you answers, insights, and help through the display.

Q: How do you handle privacy?
A: We take your privacy seriously. Your conversations are never used for training or sold. All audio is instantly and permanently deleted, and only the transcription is saved for your use.

Q: How does this compare to Ray-Ban Meta AI Glasses?
A: Mira glasses can listen to your entire day, helping you to remember everything, and assisting you in real time, in a personalized way. Meta's glasses only help when you directly ask, and doesn't know your history or preferences.

Q: What is the return policy?
A: We offer a 14-day return policy if you don't like them or there's a functionality issue. However, prescription lenses are non-refundable and non-reworkable once ordered, as they are custom-made specifically for your prescription.

Q: What languages can I translate?
A: MIRA supports 60+ languages including Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bosnian, Bulgarian, Catalan, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Estonian, Finnish, French, Galician, German, Greek, Gujarati, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Korean, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malay, Malayalam, Marathi, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Vietnamese, and Welsh.

Q: What colors does it come in?
A: Any color you want, as long as it's Black.

Q: Is this product HSA eligible?
A: Not yet, but we're working on it. Our team is actively pursuing HSA eligibility. While we can't give an exact date, making this product HSA-eligible is a priority. You can still purchase it with a regular debit or credit card today, and we'll update this page as soon as HSA eligibility is available.

Q: When will my order ship?
A: Batch 1 ships at the end of January. Batch 2 non-prescription ships end of February. Prescription orders ship in early April. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking details as soon as your order is on its way.

Q: Is there a subscription to use the glasses?
A: The core features are unlimited and free, including unlimited meeting transcription, note-taking, translation, and more. We have an optional tier for unlimited AI assistant access for $20 per month. Early customers receive a one-month trial of unlimited AI queries.

Q: Do you support Android?
A: We only support iOS right now. We don't have a timeline nailed down yet for the Android app, but it should be in the next few months.
