## Questions to Ask Every Provider

When requesting quotes, include these specifics:

**Product specs:**
- Smart glasses + charging case (lithium-ion battery, ~500mAh) + smart ring
- Total weight: ~0.2 kg per order
- Declared value: \$500
- Class 9 Dangerous Goods — requires UN 38.3 compliance

**Volume:**
- 200-1,000 orders/month (variable, growing)
- Restock frequency: monthly or bi-monthly

**Requirements:**
- Shopify integration (real-time inventory sync, auto-fulfillment)
- Lithium battery compliant storage and shipping
- US return address and returns handling
- 2-5 day domestic delivery

**Ask specifically:**
1. What is your monthly minimum commitment?
2. Do you handle Class 9 Dangerous Goods (lithium batteries)? What are the surcharges?
3. Can you provide an itemized quote (receiving, storage, pick/pack, domestic shipping, freight, customs)?
4. Do you offer China-side QC or inspection services?
5. What is your onboarding timeline?
6. Can you provide 2-3 references from electronics/hardware brands?
