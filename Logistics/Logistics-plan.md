# Mira Logistics Plan — Freight Forwarding & Fulfillment

**Product:** Mira Glasses + MiraCase (charging case, Li-ion) + Mira Ring
**Monthly volume:** 200-1,000 units (variable, growing)
**Origin:** Shenzhen → US (DTC ecommerce via Shopify)
**Special handling:** Class 9 Dangerous Goods (lithium-ion battery, ~500mAh)

---

## Strategy: Three Phases

### Phase 1: Sample Production
**You handle QC personally in Shenzhen + DDP per-parcel shipping**

- You are on the ground in Shenzhen during sample production, inspecting units yourself at the factory
- 100% inspection — you personally verify every sample unit and coordinate directly with the manufacturer on defects and iterations
- Ship approved units via DDP carrier (YunExpress/4PX) direct to US customers
- No US inventory, no 3PL, no freight forwarder — ship per order as orders come in
- Use this phase to build your QC checklist: pogo pin alignment, charging function, hinge feel, lens protection, ring dock fit, battery function, LED indicators, cosmetic finish

**Upfront capital:** ~\$0
**Delivery speed:** 7-15 days
**US returns:** No (refund & keep)
**Timeline:** Production runs 1-3 (~3-6 months)

### Phase 2: Early Mass Production
**Dedicated QC contractor in Shenzhen + Integrated FF/3PL provider**

- You can no longer be in Shenzhen full-time — hire a dedicated QC contractor in SZ (~\$400-800/mo) to inspect 100% of units
- QC contractor receives units from the factory, runs your checklist, rejects defective units, and coordinates fixes with the manufacturer
- QC-approved units are bulk-shipped (air freight) to a US warehouse via your FF/3PL provider
- Provider handles: customs, duties, warehousing, pick/pack, Shopify fulfillment
- Customers receive orders in 2-5 days via USPS/UPS
- US return address through the provider

**Upfront capital:** Bulk inventory purchase + duties for first shipment
**QC cost:** ~\$400-800/mo (dedicated SZ contractor)
**Delivery speed:** 2-5 days
**US returns:** Yes (via provider)
**Timeline:** Runs 4-8 (~6-12 months)
**Graduation criteria to Phase 3:** 3 consecutive production runs with <2% defect rate

### Phase 3: Mature Mass Production
**Third-party inspection firm replaces dedicated QC contractor + Integrated FF/3PL provider**

- Replace your SZ QC contractor with a pre-shipment inspection firm (HQTS, Pro QC International) on a sampling basis
- Sampling-based inspection: firm visits factory every 3-5 shipments, pulls random units, runs your checklist, sends pass/fail report with photos
- FF/3PL provider continues to handle everything from SZ pickup to US customer delivery
- Monitor for quality fade: material substitution, component downgrade, dimensional drift

**QC cost:** ~\$200-400 per inspection (not per month)
**Delivery speed:** 2-5 days
**US returns:** Yes (via provider)
**Timeline:** Run 9+ (ongoing)

---

## 5 Key Decision Factors

Since duties dominate costs and make per-order pricing nearly identical across providers, the real differentiators are:

- **Delivery speed** — 2-5 days vs 7-15 days. At \$650 retail, slow shipping kills conversion.
- **Quality control** — Someone inspects before the customer opens the box, or nobody does. One bad unit = one devastating review.
- **Upfront capital** — \$0 vs ~\$100K+. Determines which options are even available to you.
- **US returns** — At \$650, "refund and keep" is too expensive and "ship back to China" is a non-starter. You need a US return address.
- **Operational complexity** — Every hour spent coordinating logistics partners is an hour not spent reducing COGS.

## Key Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Flexport \$5K/mo minimum in slow months | Budget for it, or start with ShipBob (lower minimums) and switch later |
| Quality fade in mature production | Never drop to zero QC; periodic third-party inspections are cheap insurance |

| Provider doesn't handle lithium batteries | Verify DG/HAZMAT compliance before signing — this is a dealbreaker |
| US warehouse loses / damages inventory | Confirm warehouse insurance policy. Start with small test shipment. |

## Questions to Ask Every Provider

When requesting quotes, include these specifics:

**Product specs:**
- Smart glasses + charging case (lithium-ion battery, ~500mAh) + smart ring
- Total weight: ~0.2 kg per order
- Class 9 Dangerous Goods — requires UN 38.3 compliance

**Volume:**
- 200-1,000 orders/month (variable, growing)
- Restock frequency: monthly or bi-monthly

**Requirements:**
- Shopify integration (real-time inventory sync, auto-fulfillment)
- Lithium battery compliant storage and shipping
- US return address and returns handling
- 2-5 day domestic delivery

**Ask specifically:**
1. What is your monthly minimum commitment?
2. Do you handle Class 9 Dangerous Goods (lithium batteries)? What are the surcharges?
3. Can you provide an itemized quote (receiving, storage, pick/pack, domestic shipping, freight, customs)?
4. Can your SZ warehouse receive QC-approved inventory from our Shenzhen operation for bulk consolidation and shipment?
5. What is your onboarding timeline?
6. Can you provide 2-3 references from electronics/hardware brands?
